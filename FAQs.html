FAQs

VIBENEST is a platform designed to transform your solo trips into shared adventures with like-minded travelers. You can join or host events, meet new people, and explore exciting destinations across Sri Lanka.

Joining an event is simple! Once you sign up, you can browse the Event Dashboard to see upcoming trips. Select the event you're interested in, view the details, and click the "Join" button. You’ll be added to the event and notified of all updates.

Yes! VIBENEST encourages users to take charge and create their own events. As you grow within the community, you'll have the option to host your own adventures and invite others to join. It's a great way to enhance your event management skills!

Some events may have associated costs, depending on the nature of the trip and activities. Event organizers will clearly list any fees or costs associated with joining. However, many events are free, and you only need to cover personal expenses like transportation and meals.

Once you’ve joined an event, you can easily reach out to the organizer through the event details page. There will be a contact button that allows you to message the organizer directly with any questions or concerns.
